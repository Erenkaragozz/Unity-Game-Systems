Unity & C# Advanced FPS Character Controller

A modular and advanced First-Person Character Controller supporting walking, running, jumping, crouching, and smooth mouse look.

Features
- Walk, run, and crouch with smooth transitions
- Jumping with gravity and grounded check
- Mouse look with adjustable sensitivity and rotation limits
- Configurable movement and physics parameters
- Modular and reusable in other Unity projects

Requirements

- Unity 2020.3 or newer  
- Compatible with HDRP or URP pipelines
